what we do

Software Engineering

The University of Ghana's Department of Computer Science emphasizes Software Engineering, guiding students through the complete software development cycle. Through a blend of theory and practice, undergraduates gain the skills needed to create robust software solutions meeting industry standards.

Artificial Intelligence

AI is a core study area at the department, exposing students to principles and applications such as natural language processing and computer vision. The curriculum integrates theory with hands-on projects, ensuring students are well-versed in AI-driven solutions with real-world impact.

Professional Learning

Machine Learning

Machine Learning is a central focus in the undergraduate program, covering algorithms, statistical models, and computational methods. The curriculum emphasizes practical applications, preparing students to address challenges in various domains. Ongoing research contributes to advancements in ML techniques.

Human Computer Interaction

HCI is a vital aspect of computer science education, focusing on the design and evaluation of user interfaces. Students learn user-centered design principles and interaction design. The department's commitment ensures graduates can create technology that is not only functional but also user-friendly and accessible.
